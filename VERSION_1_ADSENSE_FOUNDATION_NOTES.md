# Version 1.0 — AdSense & Content Quality Foundation

This build adds:

- A site-wide trust/legal footer.
- Editorial Policy, Fact-Checking Policy, Corrections Policy and Source Policy.
- Advertising Policy, Affiliate Disclosure and Cookie Policy.
- Category-aware long-form rule guidance with practical scenarios, traveller-specific advice and packing checklists.
- A post-build AdSense-readiness validator that checks trust pages, rule-template quality features and sitemap coverage.

## Important

This release improves readiness but does not guarantee Google AdSense approval. Approval remains Google's decision and depends on the live site's overall content, policy compliance, traffic quality, navigation and user experience.
