# Version 2.2 — Rule Page Depth Expansion

This release adds a substantial category-aware reading section to every rule page.

## Added

- In-depth guidance for Batteries, Medication, Baby travel, Liquids, Cosmetics, Electronics and Food & customs.
- Separate cabin-baggage and checked-baggage explanations.
- Airport screening and connecting-flight advice.
- Destination and customs considerations.
- Common mistakes and practical journey examples.
- Status-aware wording based on each rule's Allowed, Restricted or Not allowed values.
- Build validation that confirms the component is integrated and every active category has dedicated guidance.

## Quality principle

The content engine does not promise that a fixed word count alone makes a page useful. It expands pages with relevant, category-specific guidance while retaining official-source reminders and the rule's individual restrictions, tips and warnings.
