# Version 3.2 — Airline & Country Intelligence

This release enriches every canonical `/airlines/[slug]/` and `/countries/[slug]/` hub with contextual travel intelligence. It intentionally avoids publishing precise baggage weights, customs thresholds or legal claims that have not been verified against a current official source.

## Included

- Airline-specific verification notes for major carriers.
- Country-specific customs and biosecurity alerts for core destinations.
- Five substantial guidance sections on every airline and country hub.
- Official-source verification checklist.
- Contextual airline, destination and item-category warnings inside Trip Checker.
- Post-build validator for hub, sitemap and Trip Checker integration.
